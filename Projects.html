Electro Adhesion Cubesat ADCS

Created a C based Low pass filter and PID controller in order to precisly determine and controll the orientation of a satilite.

SAAS Rebuild
The electroadhesion cube satilite is tiny satelite with the purpose of collecting small debris located in and around low earth orbit. This cube satilite recieved funding from NASA through the NASA Minds compitition. The ADCS subsystem is a sub unit of the satelite responsible for detecting and controlling the orientation of the satelite. To determine the orientation of the satelite, a arduino uno in combination with a mpu 6050 was used which posseses a gyroscope. To attinuate high frequency noise that is inherent with the mpu 6050's gyroscope, a C based butterworth low pass filter is used. To control the direction the satilite is pointed, a reaction wheel is used. The reaction wheel motor is a small brushless motor with a integrated halonomic sensor and speed controller. Precise speed control is achieved with a nested PID C based control loop: One coupled with the halonomic sensor to control the speed of the inertia wheel and another coupled with the mpu 6050 to control the rotational velocity of the entire cube satelite.

Metamorphic Virus Paper

Research paper investigating and eventually simulating the complex properties of metamorphic viruses using OOP Java.

SAAS Rebuild
A metamorphic virus is a collection of malware with the capability to transform the code that generates the malware from generation to generation, as the malware propogates throughout the computer. In my research paper, I first identify the most common techniques by which these viruses transform themselves. I then simulate the virus and the host files using java and intentionally 'infect' the simulated files with a simulated metamorphic virus possesing one of the several metamorphic transformations I have identified. I then perform numerical anylisis in order to determine the strengths and weaknesses of these transforamtions.
